But hey, life isn't all about graphs and codes, right?

## Diving Deep into Research
<i class="fas fa-rocket"></i>
In the expansive realm of digital landscapes, I find myself deeply immersed in the captivating world of graph analytics! <i class="fas fa-chart-network"></i> My research revolves around leveraging the immense computational power of GPUs, exploring the synergy between heterogeneity and the SYCL programming model. <i class="fas fa-laptop-code">

Beyond this, I'm venturing into the intriguing domain of frequency scaling on GPUs, pushing the boundaries of high-performance computing to new heights.

As a PhD student at the University of Salerno <i class="fas fa-graduation-cap"></i>, I'm on an exhilarating journey to unravel these cutting-edge technologies' mysteries and shape the future of computational science.
